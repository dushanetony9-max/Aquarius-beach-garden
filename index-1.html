Contact Us →

The kitchen ends where the garden begins.

Our herbs, citrus, and half the vegetables on your plate grow twenty steps from where you're sitting. The fish comes in each morning off the same boats we can see from the deck. Nothing travels far before it gets to you, and the menu shifts with the tide and the season rather than a printed page.

AQUARIUS Beach Garden was built around one idea: dining should feel like the best parts of being outside — bright, unhurried, and a little bit barefoot.
